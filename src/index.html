What is Dead Matter?

Dead Matter by Quantum Integrity Inc. is our most ambicious project yet. Bringing together all the best elements of the open-world survival genre, Dead Matter is an exansive game set in the post-apoclypic world of Alberta, Cananda. Dead Matter is unlike anything else you've seen in the survival genre, no more broken promises, abandoned development, or stale content.
